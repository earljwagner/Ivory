Publications

The following are papers about Ivory or describing algorithms implemented in Ivory:

  • Michael Bendersky and Donald Metzler and W. Bruce Croft. Learning Concept Importance Using a Weighted Dependence Model. Proceedings of the Third ACM International Conference on Web Search and Data Mining (WSDM 2010), pages 31-40, 2010, New York, New York.
  • Lidan Wang, Jimmy Lin, and Donald Metzler. Learning to Efficiently Rank. Proceedings of the 33rd Annual International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval (SIGIR 2010), pages 138-145, 2010, Geneva, Switzerland.

  • Lidan Wang, Jimmy Lin, and Donald Metzler. A Cascade Ranking Model for Efficient Ranked Retrieval. Proceedings of the 34th Annual International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval (SIGIR 2011), page 105-114, July 2011, Beijing, China.
